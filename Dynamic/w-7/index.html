Research

I work on natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning (ML). Current interests include:

  • Machine reasoning and generation with large (language/multimodal) models: compositional generation, reasoning, and control.
  • Understanding and improving large language/multi-modal models: evaluation, uncertainty, and benchmarking.
  • AI/LLM for science, engineering, and health: interdisciplinary applications.
