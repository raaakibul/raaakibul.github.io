Research

I work on natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning (ML). Current interests include:

  • Machine reasoning and generation with large (language/multimodal) models: e.g., compositional constrained generation, reasoning, and inference-time control.
  • Understanding and improving large language/multi-modal models: e.g., attack and defense, evaluation, and benchmarking.
  • AI/LLM for science, engineering, health: interdisciplinary collaboration with domain experts.
