Research Interests & Themes

My research interests lie in Machine Learning and Deep Learning-based Human-Computer Interaction Systems. That can be implemented in real-world scenarios. I am particularly fascinated by the potential of AI to enhance human life and solve complex problems across various domains.

Research Themes

Intelligent and Adaptive User Interfaces

Developing ML- and DL-based systems that automatically adapt the interface layout, content, or interaction style to match user preferences, goals, and behavior.

AI Personalization

Developing techniques to adapt ML models to individual users while maintaining privacy and efficiency on resource-constrained devices.

Multimodal Human Sensing and Interaction

Integrating visual, auditory, and physiological signals to improve interaction understanding and naturalness.

Cognitive and Behavioral Modeling

Modeling human attention, decision-making, and cognitive states through ML/DL. Attention prediction in complex user tasks.

Research Areas

Machine Learning

Developing novel algorithms for deep learning, reinforcement learning, and unsupervised learning to push the boundaries of AI capabilities.

Computer Vision

Creating advanced computer vision systems for object detection, image segmentation, and video analysis with real-world applications.

Artificial Intelligence

Building state-of-the-art AI models for text understanding, generation, and translation to enhance human-computer interaction.

Deep Learning

Integrating AI with robotics to create autonomous systems that can operate in complex environments and assist humans.

AI Ethics & Safety

Researching ethical implications of AI and developing frameworks for safe, fair, and transparent artificial intelligence systems.

Applied Machine Learning

Applying machine learning to medical diagnosis, drug discovery, and personalized treatment planning to improve healthcare outcomes.
